Sun Nation will be performing at [Malvern Rocks Festival](https://malvern.rocks/) on Saturday 29th July 2023. You can see them from 2:00pm to 3:00pm at the Red Lion Inn, St Ann's Road, Great Malvern WR14 4RG.

Malvern Rocks is a multi-stage, multi-venue music festival featuring a wide variety of musical genres, from rock and pop to folk and blues. There will be performances on multiple stages in around ten venues.
